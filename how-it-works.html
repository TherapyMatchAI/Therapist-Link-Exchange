How the Exchange Works

We use a 3-way (ABC) model to avoid direct swaps and keep the network stable.

A links to B → B links to C → C links back to A. No direct swaps.

1) The ABC model

A → B → C → A. You link to your assigned match; a different therapist links back to you. No 1:1 trades.

2) Two links on day one

During onboarding, you’ll place two links: your assigned match, plus a foundational resource we rotate into every new chain to keep loops stable and reduce breakage.

3) Maintain one link

After onboarding, you only maintain one outbound link to your active match. If a site drops, we’ll re-pair the chain.

Benefits of Earning Links

  • ↑
    Higher visibility in Google — quality links help your pages rank for service + location terms.
  • ★
    Domain authority growth — steady links from real practices improve trust signals over time.
  • 👥
    Referral traffic — visitors click through from partner resource pages and find your services.
  • 🔗
    Safer than direct swaps — the ABC structure avoids obvious reciprocal patterns.
  • ⚖️
    Balanced & curated — pairings are moderated by specialty and geography for relevance.
  • ⏱
    Low maintenance — after onboarding, you maintain just one outbound link.
Note: Participation is therapist-only. We curate submissions and may adjust pairings to keep chains balanced by location/specialty.
