How the Exchange Works

We use a 3-way (ABC) model to avoid direct swaps and keep the network stable.

A links to B → B links to C → C links back to A. No direct swaps.

1) The ABC model

A → B → C → A. You link to your assigned match; a different therapist links back to you. No 1:1 trades.

2) Two links on day one

During onboarding, you’ll place two links: your assigned match, plus a foundational resource we rotate into each new chain to keep loops stable.

3) Maintain one link

After onboarding, you only maintain one outbound link to your active match. If a site drops, we’ll re-pair the chain.

Benefits of Earning Links

  • ↑
    Higher local rankings — more visibility for service + city terms.
  • ★
    Authority growth — steady, relevant links build trust over time.
  • 👥
    Referral traffic — real clicks from partner resource pages.
  • 🔗
    Safer than direct swaps — ABC structure avoids obvious reciprocity.
  • ⚖️
    Balanced pairings — curated by specialty and geography.
  • ⏱
    Low maintenance — after onboarding, keep just one link.
Note: Participation is therapist-only. We curate submissions and may adjust pairings to keep chains balanced by location/specialty.
